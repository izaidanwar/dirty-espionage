    📋 GAME RULES

    🎯 Objective

    3-5 players. One player is secretly the Imposter. The rest are Agents.

    📝 Word Pairs

    Each round, a pair of similar words is chosen (e.g. "Coffee" vs "Latte"). Agents receive one word, the Imposter gets the other. Nobody knows who has which word!

    ✍️ Writing Rounds

    Over 5 rounds, each player writes one sentence per round that proves they know their secret word — without being too obvious!

    🗳️ Voting

    After all rounds, players read everyone's sentences and vote for who they think is the Imposter (the one with the different word).

    🏆 Scoring

    • Agents: +2 if both you AND your teammate vote correctly. +1 if only you vote correctly.
    • Imposter: +2 if NO ONE votes for you. +1 if only 1 person does. 0 if caught by 2+.

    💡 Tips

    Be clever! Write something vague enough that the Imposter can't figure out the word, but specific enough that your teammates know you're legit.
